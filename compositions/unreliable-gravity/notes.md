# Unreliable Gravity — Notes

## Concept

A slow, dark chamber piece where shifting non-functional harmonies and fragmentary motifs make the tonal centre feel like it keeps appearing and slipping away while guitar and strings circle it without resolving.

## Andrew Hill Traits

- Unstable tonal gravity
- Non-functional harmonic shifts (roots by m3 or tritone)
- Motivic fragmentation
- Asymmetrical rhetoric
- Angular but lyrical interior writing

## Development Ideas

- Guitar–violin contrapuntal exchange in section C
- Cello register leap before climax
- Violin harmonics at section transitions
- Guitar harmonic dyad fade at ending
