# Wyble Arrangement Notes — Labyrinth of Quiet Motions

## Source file used

`compositions/labyrinth-of-quiet-motions/musicxml/V3_Labyrinth_of_Quiet_Motions_Master.musicxml`

## Reason for creating the arrangement

To translate the polyphonic labyrinth into Wyble two-line guitar. The piece already has dense polyphony; the Wyble arrangement reduces guitar to two clear voices that weave through the labyrinth without simplifying the overall structure.

## Overview of the contrapuntal guitar approach

- **Upper voice:** Inner motion; 3rd, 7th of Em (G, D).
- **Lower voice:** Root and 5th (E, B); architectural bass.
- **Labyrinthine:** Two lines imply the harmonic path; strings support.
- **78 bpm:** Architectural pacing.

## Rehearsal letter structure

- **A** — Intro / labyrinth entry
- **B** — Development
- **C** — Thematic return
- **D** — Outro

## Performance suggestions

- **Clarity:** Two lines must remain clear; avoid muddiness.
- **Inner motion:** Wyble lines should feel like paths through the labyrinth.
- **Strings:** Dense polyphony; guitar adds two distinct voices.
