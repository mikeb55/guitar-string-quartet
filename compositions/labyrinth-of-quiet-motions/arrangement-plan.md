# Arrangement Plan — Labyrinth of Quiet Motions

## Instrument Roles

| Instrument | Role |
|------------|------|
| **Guitar** | Polyphonic catalyst; double-stops, triadic fragments, inner-voice movement |
| **Violin I** | Primary melodic contour; exposed upper narrative |
| **Violin II** | Counterline shadowing; rhythmic echo; motivic transfer |
| **Viola** | Inner-voice tension; chromatic glue; contrary motion |
| **Cello** | Slow bass motion; pivot tones; harmonic anchor |

## Climax Logic

~70% through piece (bars 55–65). Maximum polyphonic density. Five independent lines. Heightened dynamic (mf). Guitar and strings equally active. No register congestion.

## Ending Logic

Gradual thinning from bar 89. Final bar: soft guitar dyad (E–B), high violin harmonic (B5), sustained cello tone (E2). No tonal resolution. Suspended, quietly unfinished.

## Density Map

- Bars 1–6: Duo (30% reduced)
- Bars 19–24: Trio (30% reduced)
- Bars 41–70: Full quintet
- Bars 71–76: Trio (30% reduced)
- Bars 89–100: Thinning to trio, duo, final gesture
