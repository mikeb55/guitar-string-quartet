# Northern Letters – Notes

## Design Intent

Floating lyrical chamber music. Not a jazz ballad. Not groove-driven. Melodic landscape unfolding slowly.

## Metheny Melody Engine

1. **Stepwise gravity** – Most motion by step. Occasional 6th or octave leaps for emotional peaks.
2. **Target tones** – Strong beats land on chord 3rd, 6th, major 7.
3. **Long phrases** – 8–16 bar melodic arcs. Single breath.

## Bacharach Harmonic Surprise Engine

- Major → minor iv
- Chromatic bass movement
- Deceptive modulation
- Major7 → minor7 colour shifts

Example flavour: Cmaj7 → Fm6 → Bb13 → Ebmaj9

## Texture

Guitar + cello duet; violin melodic lead over string pad; guitar answering phrases; full quintet lyrical expansion; thin closing texture.
