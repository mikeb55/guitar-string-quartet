# Northern Letters – Arrangement Plan

## Instrument Roles

- **Guitar:** Melodic catalyst. Sustained lyrical notes, gentle dyads, light answering phrases. No dense comping.
- **Violin I:** Carries main melodic arcs. Highest register at climax.
- **Violin II:** Sustained harmonic colour, slow voice-leading.
- **Viola:** Inner harmonic glue, occasional counter-melody.
- **Cello:** Bass contour, sustained support. Duet with guitar in A.

## Texture Map

- **A:** Guitar + cello duet; guitar introduces theme
- **B:** Violin melodic lead over string pad
- **C:** Full quintet; guitar answering phrases
- **D:** Climax – longest phrase, highest violin, guitar harmonic support
- **E:** Thin closing – guitar harmonic, violin sustained, cello stepwise resolution

## Ending

Melody drifting away. No strong cadence. Guitar harmonic, violin sustained note, cello stepwise resolution.
