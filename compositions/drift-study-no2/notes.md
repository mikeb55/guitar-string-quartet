# Notes — Drift Study No.2

## Engine

Scofield–Holland Hybrid.

## Scofield Elements

- Syncopated rhythmic gestures
- Dyads and triads in guitar
- Angular melodic fragments

## Holland Elements

- Strong harmonic grounding
- Cello driving harmonic motion
- Contrapuntal ensemble movement

## Harmonic Palette

Dm9, G13sus, Bbmaj7#11, Fmaj9 — modern jazz modal with chromatic colour.
