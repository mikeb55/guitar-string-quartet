# Parallel Currents – Notes

## Design Intent

Chamber piece driven by two independent guitar voices. Harmony arises from their interaction, not block chords.

## Wyble Principle

- Upper voice: melodic line
- Lower voice: bass / counterline
- Motion: contrary, oblique, occasional parallel
- Intervals: 3rds, 6ths, contrary-motion dyads, fourths

## Rhythmic Relationship

Staggered entries, displacement, delayed resolution. Avoid strict parallelism between the two voices.
