# Notes — Home Engine

## Engine

Scofield–Holland Groove Engine. Secondary: Zappa Disruption (limited).

## Primary Inspiration

John Scofield + Dave Holland ECM album "Memories of Home".

## Core Traits

- Groove-based harmony
- Syncopated guitar language
- Strong bass movement
- Active ensemble dialogue

The piece should feel alive and rhythmically forward-moving, not floating.

## Zappa Placement

Section D only. Ensemble hits, rhythmic fractures, unexpected harmonic stack. Return to groove in A'.
