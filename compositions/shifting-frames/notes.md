# Shifting Frames – Notes

## Design Intent

Rhythm-driven chamber piece. Harmony secondary to rhythm. Steve Coleman–style rhythmic logic.

## Core Cell

3 + 3 + 2 subdivision: | X . . | X . . | X . |

Transformations: displacement, augmentation, compression, inversion.

## Polymetric System

- Guitar: 5-beat phrasing cycle
- Cello: 7-beat phrasing cycle
- Violins: rotating 3+3+2 cell
- Downbeats realign periodically

## Density Levels

1. Sparse rhythmic fragments
2. Medium layered rhythms
3. Dense polymetric lattice (near 70%)
