# Long Echo – Notes

## Design Intent

Final atmospheric epilogue of the Orbit Album. Dissolves tension rather than builds it. Silence as structural element. No narrative, no groove.

## Harmonic Fields

1. **Field 1:** Major + #11 / suspended colour (Dmaj7#11, Emaj7sus, etc.)
2. **Field 2:** Ambiguous mixed-mode (modal mixture, common-tone drift)
3. **Field 3:** Open unresolved final (no cadence, suspended sonority)

## Texture Targets

- 35% reduced texture (guitar + cello, trio subsets, near-silence)
- Refresh every 8–12 bars
- Silence as structural device

## Dynamic Range

pp, p, mp only. One brief mf crest maximum. Long crescendi/decrescendi across measures.
