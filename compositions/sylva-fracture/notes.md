# Notes — Sylva Fracture

## Concept

Dramatic narrative piece. Wayne Shorter primary: motif development, harmonic narrative, non-functional progressions. Zappa secondary: only 1–3 moments of unexpected ensemble hits, rhythmic disruption, angular voicings, sudden dynamics.

## Motif

F#–A–B–D (4 notes, Lydian flavor). Transform through:
- Inversion
- Interval expansion
- Rhythmic displacement
- Register displacement

## Zappa Placement

Section E (fractured gesture) — allow 1–3 Zappa-style moments:
- Unexpected tutti hit
- Rhythmic disruption (off-beat accent)
- Sudden dynamic contrast (ff)

Core character remains Wayne Shorter / ECM chamber music.
