# Rhythm and Phrase Rules

Rhythmic behaviour for this project.

## Asymmetrical Phrase Lengths

- Prefer 3, 5, 7 bars over constant 4+4
- Phrase length can vary within a section
- Avoid mechanical symmetry unless intentionally subverted

## Breath Between Gestures

- Include rests between phrases
- Allow space before re-entry
- Silence as part of the phrase

## Phrase Spillover

- Melodic lines can cross bar lines irregularly
- Phrases need not align with 4-bar grid
- Resolution can be delayed across bar boundaries

## Delayed Resolution

- Harmonic or melodic resolution can arrive late
- Anticipation and suspension across beats
- Avoid always resolving on beat 1

## Rubato-Like Elasticity Within Notation

- Notation should allow interpretive flexibility
- Avoid notation that implies mechanical execution
- Tempo markings can suggest rubato (e.g. "poco rubato")

## Avoid

- Constant 4-bar phrases
- Every phrase starting on beat 1
- Mechanical rhythmic patterns
- Grid-locked phrasing
