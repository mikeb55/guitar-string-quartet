# Anti-Generic Rules

Explicitly prohibited patterns.

## Prohibited

### Constant Whole-Note String Pads

Strings sustaining block chords for entire sections. Every part playing long notes in lockstep. Replace with subset writing, independent motion, or rest.

### Every Instrument Playing Every Bar

Full quintet active throughout. Replace with entries and exits; allow instruments to rest; use subset textures.

### Formulaic Cinematic Harmony

- Endless iv–I or similar progressions
- Predictable emotional swells
- Generic "film score" chord progressions

### Mechanical Phrase Symmetry

- Constant 4+4 bar phrasing
- Every phrase starting on beat 1
- Predictable repetition

## Enforcement

Before output, check:
- [ ] No section has all five instruments playing whole notes for more than 2 bars
- [ ] At least one section uses 2–3 instruments only
- [ ] Phrase lengths vary (not all 4 bars)
- [ ] Harmony is melody-driven, not formulaic
