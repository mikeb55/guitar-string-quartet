# Structural Evolution Engine

Slow chamber compositions risk monotony if harmonic or textural ideas repeat too long.

## Mandatory Structural Requirements

Each composition must contain:

- **5–7 distinct textures**
- **3 harmonic fields**
- **2 ensemble-hit sections**
- **1 contrast section**
- **1 transformed return**

## Variation Thresholds

- harmonic progression may not repeat more than 4 bars without alteration
- rhythmic cell may not repeat more than 3 times consecutively
- motif must evolve after 2–3 iterations
- texture must change every 8–12 bars

## Required Variation Mechanisms

- register shift
- orchestration change
- harmonic colour shift
- rhythmic transformation
- melodic inversion or expansion
- ensemble accent or silence
- dynamic contrast

If these thresholds are violated the section must be rewritten.
