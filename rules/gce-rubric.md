# GCE Rubric

Internal quality evaluation for compositional output. Score from 0–10. **Only accept outputs ≥ 9.0** unless explicitly disabled.

## Criteria

### 1. Melodic Identity (0–10)

- Distinct, memorable material
- No generic contours or predictable patterns
- Lyrical when appropriate; angular when intentional
- **9+:** Melody is unmistakably this piece; no AI-generic feel

### 2. Harmonic Clarity (0–10)

- Melody-driven harmony
- Modal colour; non-root support when used
- Clear voice-leading
- **9+:** Harmony supports and enhances; never generic or formulaic

### 3. Ensemble Interaction (0–10)

- Chamber dialogue; not tutti accompaniment
- Instruments respond to each other
- Call and response; conversational
- **9+:** Feels like five players in conversation

### 4. Voice Independence (0–10)

- Each part has its own logic
- No lockstep doubling
- Contrapuntal awareness
- **9+:** Every part is worth playing alone

### 5. Texture Control (0–10)

- Subset writing; transparency
- Register separation
- Appropriate density per section
- **9+:** Texture serves the music; no constant full ensemble

### 6. Emotional Resonance (0–10)

- Restraint; silence; understatement
- Intensity through control
- Avoids generic "emotional" gestures
- **9+:** Genuine emotional content without cliché

## Scoring

- **Total:** Average of six criteria (or weighted if specified)
- **Threshold:** ≥ 9.0 to accept
- **If below 9.0:** Revise until threshold met, or flag for explicit override

## Application

Apply this rubric before finalising any generated material. Use `prompts/revision-template.md` when revisions are needed.
