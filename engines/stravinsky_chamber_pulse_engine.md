# Stravinsky Chamber Pulse Engine

## Purpose

Generate rhythmically energetic chamber pieces inspired by Stravinsky's chamber writing but adapted for jazz harmony.

## Influences

- Stravinsky chamber music
- modern chamber jazz
- ECM rhythmic ensemble writing

## Core Characteristics

- rhythmic cells
- shifting meters
- sudden ensemble gestures
- motivic rhythmic development

## Harmony Style

- modal harmony
- quartal harmony
- polychord colours
- occasional sharp dissonance

**Avoid** romantic harmonic language.

## Rhythmic Behaviour

- repeating rhythmic cells
- irregular phrase lengths
- syncopated ensemble hits
- rhythmic dialogue between instruments

## Instrumentation Roles

| Instrument | Role |
|------------|------|
| **Violin I** | rhythmic motif leader |
| **Violin II** | counter-rhythm layer |
| **Viola** | inner rhythmic motor |
| **Cello** | percussive harmonic anchor |
| **Guitar** | dyads, rhythmic chords, percussive attacks |

## Structural Tendency

- **Intro** — rhythmic cell
- **A** — development
- **B** — contrasting section
- **A'** — recombined cell patterns
- **Coda** — rhythmic dissolution
