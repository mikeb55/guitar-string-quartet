# Comparative Models

Reference points for this project’s aesthetic territory.

## Primary: Bill Frisell String Work

- **Richter 858** — Spacious, sustained, transparent; string quartet with guitar/electronics
- **Sign of Life** — Chamber jazz; guitar integrated; modal colour
- **Big Sur** — Landscape quality; slow development; independent voices
- **When You Wish Upon a Star** — Lyrical simplicity; sparse accompaniment

## Secondary: ECM Chamber Aesthetic

- Transparent orchestration
- Restraint and silence
- Modal harmony
- Lyrical melody over sparse texture

## What We Are Not Modelling

- Classical string quartet repertoire (different role for guitar)
- Cinematic underscore (different density and function)
- Jazz big band (different orchestration)
- Pop string arrangements (different harmonic language)

## Application

When in doubt, ask: *Would this fit on Richter 858 or Sign of Life?* If not, revise toward transparency, restraint, and chamber interplay.
